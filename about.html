Our Mission & Vision

Ecovision is more than just a software solution; it is a commitment to the nation's mission of **Atmanirbhar Bharat** (self-sustained India). Our platform utilizes locally developed technology (**Swadeshi** principle) to tackle the pressing environmental challenges of urban and rural India.

We envision a future where waste is seen not as a liability, but as a resource, efficiently managed at the source through smart, scalable, and sustainable digital tools.
